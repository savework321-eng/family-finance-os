# Product Vision

## Vision

To become a modern, reliable, and sustainable family financial operating system that helps every family manage their finances with clarity, discipline, and confidence.

---

## Mission

- Provide a centralized financial management platform for the entire family.
- Simplify daily financial recording.
- Encourage healthy budgeting habits.
- Monitor assets, liabilities, and net worth.
- Support long-term financial planning.
- Deliver clear and meaningful financial reports.
- Build a scalable system that can evolve over many years.

---

## Long-Term Goals

### Version 1.0

- Dashboard
- Transactions
- Budget
- Goals
- Assets
- Liabilities
- Reports
- Family Consolidation

---

### Future Versions

- Mobile Friendly PWA
- Bank Integration
- OCR Receipt Scanner
- AI Financial Assistant
- Investment Tracking
- Multi Currency
- Family Calendar Integration

---

## Product Principles

- Easy to Use
- Fast
- Secure
- Accurate
- Transparent
- Modular
- Scalable

---

## Success Indicators

The project will be considered successful when:

- Every family member can manage their own finances.
- The owner can monitor overall family finances.
- Monthly financial reports can be generated automatically.
- Financial goals can be tracked effectively.
- The system remains maintainable for many years.
